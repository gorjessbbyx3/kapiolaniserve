# KapiolaniServe — Photo Shot List

A practical list of exactly what photos are still needed, where each one goes, and how to frame it. Hand this to Luna's team or a student photographer — no design background needed.

**General specs for all photos:**
- Landscape orientation unless noted otherwise
- Good natural light — outdoor/window light beats indoor fluorescent every time
- Real people doing real things > posed group photos looking at the camera
- Horizontal phone photos are fine — nothing here needs a "real" camera
- Send originals uncropped; cropping happens on our end

---

## Priority 1 — Highest visual impact

### 1. Māla Māunuunu (campus garden)
- **Where it goes:** Home page campus programs row, and possibly the Environment pathway hero
- **Shot:** Someone actively working in the garden — weeding, planting, or doing the Hawaiian dry-stack stonework. Wide shot showing the garden setting (behind the Mānele building), not just a close-up of hands.
- **Aspect:** 4:3 or landscape
- **Bonus:** A few close-ups of specific native plants in bloom would work well as smaller accent images later.

### 2. Pathway hero photos (7 needed — one per pathway)
Each pathway page (`/pathways/[slug]`) has one placeholder for a representative photo. These don't need to be from a specific partner site — just something that captures the *feel* of that pathway:

| Pathway | Suggested shot |
|---|---|
| Environment | Restoration work day, planting, or a shot at Māla Māunuunu / Diamond Head |
| Health | A campus blood drive, or the Health Promotion Team tabling |
| Education | A tutoring session or classroom moment (with permission from the school/site) |
| Arts, History & Culture | A student at a cultural site — ʻIolani Palace, a museum, anywhere on the partner list |
| Intercultural Perspectives & Identities | The International Café or a language-exchange moment |
| Bridging Generations | A student with a kūpuna — a visit, a shared activity, anything warm and candid |
| Resilience Neighborhoods | A food-pantry moment, a prep-kit assembly, or a community event |

- **Aspect:** 16:9 landscape (these sit in a wide banner-style frame)
- **Note:** If a specific photo isn't available for a pathway yet, even one good generic photo from that pathway's general activity type works as a placeholder better than nothing.
